About Nexus of Things

"Nexus of Things" is a premier technical fest organized by the CSE-IoT Department of RVR & JC College of Engineering as part of Colorido 2025. This event brings together the brightest minds to showcase their technical prowess and innovative thinking.

Event Overview

Spanning two days on December 26-27, 2025, Nexus of Things features four challenging technical events designed to test participants' skills in web development, IoT prototyping, creative problem-solving, and debugging.

What to Expect

  • Hands-on technical challenges
  • Real-world problem statements
  • Exciting prizes and recognition
  • Learning and skill development opportunities

Why Participate?

Nexus of Things provides an excellent platform for students to demonstrate their technical abilities, teamwork, and innovative approach to solving real-world problems. It's not just a competition, but a learning experience that bridges the gap between academic knowledge and practical implementation.
